About NeuroAid

NeuroAid is a project dedicated to providing an accessible and supportive learning environment for individuals with dyslexia. Our goal is to offer simple, effective tools and engaging activities that make learning more manageable and fun.

Key Features

  • Read Aloud: A powerful text-to-speech feature that reads content aloud, helping to improve phonological awareness and comprehension.
  • Letter Shuffle: A unique tool that simulates the dyslexia reading experience by dynamically shuffling letters within words to foster empathy and understanding.
  • Engaging Games: Fun, interactive games designed to strengthen cognitive skills essential for reading, memory, and focus.

Support & Community

You are not alone on this journey. While NeuroAid provides helpful tools, professional guidance is always recommended for a comprehensive assessment.

For more information and resources, please contact us at support@neuroaid.dev.
